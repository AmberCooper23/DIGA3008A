The Essay Brief

For many of us, our first (and sometimes only) impressions of a country come from the results of a search engine. However, a lot of countries are not afforded the same infrastructure and digital architecture as others, leading to an uprising of non-local search engine results in countries that are not well-connected in the digital world.
